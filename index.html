If you are new to Quakertown Eye Associates - WELCOME! To our patients - both old and new, we strive to earn your trust and we truly appreciate your business. Our goal is to keep you and your family happy and healthy! Quakertown Eye Associates, P.C. is the privately owned family eye care practice to go to. Here you will find caring doctors and staff, who strive to give you the best possible care, in a friendly & inviting atmosphere. We take the time to get to know you and truly listen. The type of individualized care we provide, is among the best in the Quakertown area!

Services

Examinations

  • Routine Eye Exams
  • Medical Eye Exams (such as Diabetic exams, cataract and dry eye evaluations)
  • Problem visits (such as red eyes and allergies)
  • Contact Lens Exams (including shperical, astigmatism and multifocal soft lenses, gas permeable contact lenses, and specialty lenses)
  • InfantSEE® Assessments (free exams for infants ages 6-12 months)
  • We do our very best to accomodate emergency exams

Glasses

Our Optical Dispensary is open during all business hours. No appointment is necessary for the purchase of glasses, frame adjustments or broken glasses issues. Because the optical operates on a first come first serve basis, there may be a wait when you arrive. We do our best to give each and every patient the time and attention that they deserve!

New Patients

Don't forget to bring your insurance information!

We accept most vision plans and most medical plans. We can not schedule your appointment without your insurance information. When you call for an appointment, please have your insurance information handy. If you do not know who your vision coverage is through, please call the number on the back of your medical insurance card, or call your human resources department, before calling our office. Once we have your information, we will verify your insurance coverage before your appointment, and call you if we discover any problems. Most vision plans do not cover your entire exam and/or materials (i.e. glasses/contacts) in full. Many vision and medical insurances have copays. You will be asked to pay these balances at the time of your exam.

MEDICARE:

We accept Medicare patients. As a courtesy, we will also bill your secondary insurance provider directly. You will be asked to sign an Advanced Beneficiary Notice for services not covered under Medicare. You shall be accountable for covering these services.
